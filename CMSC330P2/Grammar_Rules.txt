
Current:
<program> → <exp> , <assigns> ;

New:
<program> → <exp> ; | <exp> ',' <assigns> ';'

<exp> -> '(' <operand> <op> <operand> ')' | '(' <operand> '!' ')'

<operand> → <literal> | <variable> | <exp> 

<op> -> '+' | '-' | '*' | '/' | '>' | '<' | '=' | '&' | '|'

<assigns> → <assigns> , <assign> | <assign>

<assign> → <variable> = <literal>


REGULAR EXPRESSIONS:

<op>           [+-*/]

<variable>        [a-zA-Z][a-zA-Z0-9]*

<literal> [0-9]+

SCRATCHPAD:

(x + (y = 2)), x = 0, y = 1;
